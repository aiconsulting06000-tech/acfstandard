Agentic Commerce Framework (ACF)

ACF is a governance methodology for autonomous agentic systems — merging rigorous standards, operational controls, and independent certification to ensure safe, auditable, and commercially aligned agent behaviour.

Why ACF

ACF introduces a traceable governance fabric for autonomous agents: policy architecture, runtime controls, scoring and certification. It is designed for regulators, enterprise risk, and platform providers who require auditable, standardised assurances.
